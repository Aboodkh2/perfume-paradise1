Secrets of choosing the perfect perfume

  1. Test the fragrance on the skin

    2. Don't buy a perfume just based on how it smells in the bottle. Try the fragrance on your skin to see how it reacts to your body's heat and unique chemistry. The fragrance on the skin can change and become more attractive.

  2. Pay attention to the seasons

    3. Your choice of perfume depends on the season of the year. Light and refreshing perfumes are suitable for the summer, while heavy and warm perfumes are suitable for the winter.

  3. Maintain diversity

    4. Don't limit yourself to just one fragrance. Keep fragrances varied and use different fragrances for different occasions and times.
